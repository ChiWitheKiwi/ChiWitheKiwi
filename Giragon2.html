Giragon

Girragon by richardnubla on Sketchfab

When tasked to create a mash of 3-D objects, I created Giragon, the devourer of worlds. He approaches the Earth looking to devour. He seems to resemble a giraffe with some aspects of a dragon. He is cloaked with the colors of the universe, so we will never see him coming.

Done in Meshmixer, 2020.
